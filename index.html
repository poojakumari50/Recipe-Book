Panipuri Vadapav Bhelpuri Burger Chowmein

1. Panipuri

Ingredients:

  • Puri (Readymade or Homemade)
  • Boiled and mashed Potatoes
  • Chopped onions
  • Chopped Chilli
  • Chopped Corainder
  • Boiled gram or peas
  • Salt / Black salt
  • Lemon
  • Boondi
  • Dahi
  • Sev
  • Chat masala
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Mango Powder
  • Chilli Powder

Process:

  1. Take boiled and mashed potato, onions, chilli, corainder leaf, gram and peas, salt and black salt, chili powder, mango powder and chat masala in a bowl.
  2. Mix them together with hand or spoon and masla is ready.
  3. Take tamarind chutney, corainder, chilli and add lemon juice and grind until smooth paste.
  4. Transfer them to a large bowl and add chat masla, chilli powder, black salt, 5cups water and stir it with spoon.
  5. If you like boondi then add it in both masala and pani.
  6. Take a puri and make a hole on its top-middle with a spoon or with finger.
  7. Stuff it with masala as much as quantity of masala you like.
  8. Put some curd with spoon over it and sprinkle sev.
  9. Dip each puri in tamarind water you prepared.
  10. Eat and Enjoy...

2. Vadapav

Ingredients:

  • Gram flour
  • Rice flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Bread rolls
  • Boiled and mashed potatoes
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Green chutney
  • Chopped onions
  • Chopped chilli
  • Chopped coriander
  • Hing
  • Mustard seeds
  • Cumin seeds

Process:

  1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, rice flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt, and water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.
  3. Dip the boiled and mashed potatoes in the batter and deep fry until golden brown. Set aside.
  4. In another pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, asafoetida, and curry leaves.
  5. Add chopped onions and green chilies. Saute until onions are golden brown.
  6. Place the fried potato balls in pav and top with the onion mixture.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with tamarind chutney and green chutney.
  8. Eat and Enjoy...

3. Bhelpuri

Ingredients:

  • puffed rice
  • sev (crisp chickpea noodles)
  • chopped tomatoes
  • chopped onions
  • chopped cilantro
  • tamarind chutney
  • mint-coriander chutney
  • boiled and diced potatoes
  • Salt
  • chaat masala
  • cumin powder
  • red chili powder

Process:

  1. In a large bowl, combine puffed rice, sev, chopped tomatoes, chopped onions, chopped cilantro, and diced potatoes.
  2. Add tamarind chutney and mint-coriander chutney. Mix well to coat the ingredients evenly.
  3. Season with salt, chaat masala, roasted cumin powder, and red chili powder. Toss everything together until well combined.
  4. Serve immediately the delicious Bhel Puri otherwise it will become soft.
  5. Eat and enjoy...

4. Burger

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Diced onions
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Hamburger buns
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Process:

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, diced onions, ketchup, mustard, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.
  3. Preheat a grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the buns and adding your favorite toppings like lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  7. Eat and Enjoy...

5. Chowmein

Ingredients:

  • Chow mein noodles
  • Vegetable oil
  • Sliced chicken or beef
  • Sliced mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, etc.)
  • Cloves garlic, minced
  • Chilli sauce
  • Tomato sauce
  • Soya sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

Process:

  1. Cook the chow mein noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add sliced chicken or beef and stir-fry until browned and cooked through.
  4. Add minced garlic and sliced mixed vegetables. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until vegetables are slightly tender.
  5. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and chicken or vegetable broth.
  6. Pour the sauce mixture over the meat and vegetables. Stir well to combine.
  7. Add the cooked chow mein noodles to the wok and toss everything together until well coated in the sauce.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired.
  10. Eat and Enjoy...
